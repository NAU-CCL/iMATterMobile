About iMATter
What is iMATter?

iMATter is an app that offers a ranges of supportive features for women in opioid recovery to maintain a social network, ask for advice from peers, and engage in fun challenges and ways to stay positive as you continue your recovery journey.


iMATter provides a supportive safety net for mothers in similar situations by creating an online community, supporting a safe and shame free sisterhood of mothers facing the same challenges.
